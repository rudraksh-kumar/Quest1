Why Communities & Real-World Exposure Matter

Students often learn in isolation with little connection to real-world work. Caarya bridges this gap through:

  • Industrial Pods → real work environments
  • Facilitators → guidance without rigid instruction
  • Ecosystem → network, collaboration, exposure

Exposure + community turns exploration into direction.
